# Retail Sales & Profitability Analysis Dashboard

This project analyzes retail transaction data to uncover insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer purchasing behavior, and payment trends.

Using Power BI, an interactive dashboard was developed to help stakeholders identify high-performing product categories, profitable branches, customer preferences, and operational patterns that support data-driven business decisions.

The analysis focuses on key business metrics such as revenue, profit, product demand, payment behavior, and branch-level performance.

## Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of transactional data every day, but without proper analysis it becomes difficult to identify which products drive profitability, which branches perform best, and how customer purchasing behavior impacts business performance.

This analysis aims to answer important business questions such as:

- Which branch generates the highest profit?
- Which product categories contribute most to overall sales?
- What payment methods are preferred by customers?
- Which product lines perform best across different cities?
- How profitable is the business overall?
- What customer purchasing patterns can be identified from the data?

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze retail transaction data and uncover insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer purchasing behavior, and product category trends using Power BI visualizations.

## Key Insights

- Branch C generated the highest overall profit, indicating stronger sales performance compared to other branches.

- Electronic accessories and Food & Beverage products were among the top-selling categories, contributing significantly to overall sales volume.

- Customer payments were fairly distributed across credit card, cash, and e-wallet methods, showing diverse payment preferences.

- The business maintained an overall profit margin of approximately 4.8%, reflecting stable profitability across transactions.

- Product category performance varied across branches and cities, highlighting differences in regional customer demand patterns.

## Business Impact

This dashboard enables businesses to monitor sales performance, identify profitable product categories, analyze customer purchasing behavior, and support data-driven decision-making. The insights derived from the analysis can help improve profitability, optimize product strategies, and better understand customer preferences across different branches and cities.

## Tools & Technologies Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX Measures
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization
- CSV Dataset

## Dataset Information

- Dataset: Retail Transaction Dataset
- Format: CSV
- Records: Approximately 1,000 retail transaction records
- Features Included: Sales, Profit, Product Line, Payment Method, Customer Ratings, Branch, and City

## Project Workflow

1. Collected and imported retail transaction data into Power BI.
2. Performed data cleaning and preprocessing using Power Query.
3. Created calculated measures and KPIs using DAX.
4. Built interactive visualizations to analyze sales, profit, customer behavior, and payment trends.
5. Generated business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

## Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing product categories to maximize revenue generation.
- Improve sales strategies in lower-performing branches to achieve more balanced profitability.
- Encourage digital payment adoption through customer offers and loyalty programs.
- Monitor low-profit product categories and optimize pricing or inventory strategies accordingly.
- Use customer purchasing trends to improve product planning and business decision-making.

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how Power BI can be used to transform raw retail transaction data into meaningful business insights. Through interactive visualizations and KPI analysis, the dashboard helps identify profitable branches, top-performing product categories, customer payment preferences, and sales trends.

The analysis supports data-driven decision-making by enabling businesses to optimize sales strategies, improve profitability, and better understand customer purchasing behavior.
